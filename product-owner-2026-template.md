# Product Owner 2026 Template
*Bridge AI, Ethics, Strategy & Delivery*

---

## 1. Your Unique 2026 PO Value Proposition
You are the only role bridging:
- AI/Tech Execution: Prioritize backlog items leveraging LLMs, automation, low-code tools
- Shifting Customer Needs: Own continuous feedback loops for AI product adoption
- Business Strategy + Ethics: Reject high-revenue, low-trust features that harm users

### Tangible Success Metrics to Prove Impact
| Metric | Target | Measurement Frequency |
|--------|--------|------------------------|
| High-impact feature delivery speed | 20%+ faster than industry benchmark | Per release |
| NPS/Retention gains from AI features | 15%+ sustained lift | Monthly |
| Stakeholder alignment on priorities | >85% | Quarterly survey |
| Backlog waste reduction | 20%+ fewer stale/low-value items | Monthly audit |

---

## 2. The 5+ Target User Rule (Mandatory for AI Products)
**Definition**: Validate all major AI features with ≥5 representative end-users (exclude internal stakeholders) pre-development.

**Why it matters for AI**:
- Catches non-deterministic AI risks (hallucinations, bias, tone mismatches) early
- Cuts 3-5x costly retraining/rework post-launch
- Protects user trust in fragile AI interactions

## 4. PO Todo Template (2026-Aligned)
### Recurring Cadence
- [ ] Weekly: Groom backlog (target 20%+ waste reduction)
- [ ] Biweekly: User feedback sessions (NPS/CSAT/retention)
- [ ] Monthly: Stakeholder priority sync (target >85% alignment)
- [ ] Quarterly: Roadmap alignment (business + AI/tech capabilities)

### Strategic Alignment
- [ ] 100% of prioritized features map to ≥1 business OKR or validated user pain point
- [ ] Monthly backlog audit: <15% low-priority/non-strategic items
- [ ] Quarterly AI tool adoption review: Adjust backlog to leverage new tech

### Delivery & Impact
- [ ] Track cycle time for high-impact features: Target [X days] to GA
- [ ] Prioritize 60%+ sprint backlog to high-impact user pain points
- [ ] Monthly reporting on 4 core outcomes (delivery speed, NPS, alignment, waste)

### Governance
- [ ] Enforce "Definition of Ready": 5+ target user validation for all AI features
- [ ] Weekly 1-line priority updates to all stakeholders
- [ ] Resolve 100% of stakeholder conflicts within 48 hours

### Ethical Ownership
- [ ] Pre-launch review: Bias, privacy, accessibility compliance
- [ ] Quarterly ethics guidelines update
- [ ] Annual 3rd party ethical audit

1. Add the 5+ User Rule to your team's Definition of Ready for all AI features
2. Track the 4 success metrics in a shared monthly dashboard
